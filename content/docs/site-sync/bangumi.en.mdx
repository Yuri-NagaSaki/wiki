---
title: Bangumi Moe Sync
description: Fill in the Cookie, publisher identity, and Team ID required by AniBT's Bangumi Moe sync.
---

## Step One

Open [https://bangumi.moe](https://bangumi.moe), log out first, and refresh the page.

Press <kbd>F12</kbd> to open DevTools, then switch to **Network**. Make sure network recording is enabled.

When the recording button is **red**, recording is enabled.

![DevTools Network recording](/images/site-sync/bangumi-network-recording.webp)

Enter your account and password, then log in.

## Step Two

In the **Network** panel, find and click the request named **`disqus`**.

![The disqus request in Network](/images/site-sync/bangumi-disqus-request.webp)

In the right panel, open **Headers** and scroll down to **Request Headers**. Copy the entire **Cookie** value.

**Do not include the `priority` value below it, such as `u=1, i`.**

![Copy the Cookie request header](/images/site-sync/bangumi-cookie-header.webp)

Return to AniBT, paste the Cookie, and save it.

![Paste the Cookie in AniBT](/images/site-sync/bangumi-anibt-cookie-save.webp)

For **Publisher Identity Name** and **Team ID**:

- **Publisher Identity Name**: enter your Bangumi Moe team name if you have one.
- **Team ID**: optional. Sync still works without it.

Save the form and test the connection.

If it shows **connection successful**, the setup is complete. If it fails, check whether the Cookie was copied completely and whether login succeeded.

![Successful connection test](/images/site-sync/bangumi-connection-success.webp)
