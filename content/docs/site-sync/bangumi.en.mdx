Bangumi Moe authenticates by login cookie. Capture it from DevTools while logging in.

<Callout type="warn">
  You **must log out and clear Network first**, then log in again — otherwise the captured request won't carry a complete cookie.
</Callout>

## Step One: Start Network Recording

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Log out and refresh

    Visit [https://bangumi.moe](https://bangumi.moe), **log out**, and **refresh** the page.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Enable network recording

    Press <kbd>F12</kbd> to open DevTools and switch to **Network**. Confirm the recording button is **red** (recording is on).

    ![DevTools Network recording](/images/site-sync/bangumi-network-recording.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Log in again

    Enter your account and password, then sign in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step Two: Copy the Cookie

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Locate the disqus request

    In the **Network** panel, find and click the request named **`disqus`**.

    ![The disqus request in Network](/images/site-sync/bangumi-disqus-request.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Copy the Cookie request header

    On the right, switch to **Headers** and scroll down to **Request Headers** → **Cookie**. Select the entire long value.

    <Callout type="warn">
      Do **not** include the `priority` line below it (e.g. `u=1, i`).
    </Callout>

    ![Copy the Cookie request header](/images/site-sync/bangumi-cookie-header.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Paste in AniBT and save

    Return to AniBT, paste the Cookie, and save.

    ![Paste the Cookie in AniBT](/images/site-sync/bangumi-anibt-cookie-save.webp)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Test the Connection

After saving, click **Test Connection**:

- **Success** — setup is complete.
- **Failure** — re-check whether the Cookie was copied completely and that login succeeded.

![Successful connection test](/images/site-sync/bangumi-connection-success.webp)
