---
title: Nyaa Sync
description: Fill in the session Cookie required by AniBT's Nyaa sync.
---

Nyaa authenticates by browser cookie. Copy the `session` value from DevTools.

<Callout type="warn">
  Nyaa cookies expire after **one month** and must be refreshed periodically. Manually prepend `session=` before saving.
</Callout>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open DevTools

    Logged in to Nyaa, press <kbd>F12</kbd> to open **DevTools**, then switch to the **Application** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Locate the session cookie

    In the sidebar, expand **Storage** → **Cookie** → `https://nyaa.si`. Click **session** in the name list — its value appears below.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Copy the cookie value

    Copy the long value:

    ```txt title="Value copied from DevTools"
    eyJfcGVybWFxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxKFCv50xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxNTQ4OTl9.axxxxg.jC7nBl0pLs08BP-gXnktlEbIxKc
    ```

    ![Nyaa session Cookie](/images/site-sync/nyaa-session-cookie.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Add the `session=` prefix and save

    When pasting into AniBT, **manually prepend** `session=`:

    ```txt title="Final value saved in AniBT"
    session=eyJfcGVybWFxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxKFCv50xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxNTQ4OTl9.axxxxg.jC7nBl0pLs08BP-gXnktlEbIxKc
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
