---
title: Nyaa Sync
description: Fill in the session Cookie required by AniBT's Nyaa sync.
---

> **Note: Nyaa Cookies are valid for only one month and need to be updated regularly.**
>
> **Before saving the Cookie, manually add the `session=` prefix.**

Press <kbd>F12</kbd> to open **DevTools**, then open **Application** from the top bar.

In the sidebar, find **Storage**, expand **Cookie**, and click **https://nyaa.si**.

Find **session** in the name list and click it. The **Cookie Value** will appear below. Copy that value.

Example:

```txt
eyJfcGVybWFxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxKFCv50xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxNTQ4OTl9.axxxxg.jC7nBl0pLs08BP-gXnktlEbIxKc
```

The final value saved in AniBT should be:

```txt
session=eyJfcGVybWFxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxKFCv50xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxNTQ4OTl9.axxxxg.jC7nBl0pLs08BP-gXnktlEbIxKc
```
