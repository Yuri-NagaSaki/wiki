---
title: DMHY Sync
description: Enter your DMHY username, password, and subtitle group name in AniBT, then save and test the connection.
---

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Edit credentials
    In AniBT sync settings, click **Edit credentials**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Enter username and password
    Enter your DMHY account username and password.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Enter subtitle group name
    Enter the subtitle group name you use on DMHY.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Save
    Click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Test connection
    Click **Test connection** and confirm it succeeds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
