---
title: ACGNX Sync
description: Fill in the UID and API Token required by AniBT's ACGNX sync.
---

Open [https://share.acgnx.se](https://share.acgnx.se), then click **發佈資源** in the top-left corner.

After entering the page, click **帳戶API設置** under **帳戶控制中心** in the sidebar.

Copy the **UID** and **API Token**, then paste them into AniBT and save.

> After dragging to copy and paste, check whether there are extra spaces before or after the UID and API Token.
