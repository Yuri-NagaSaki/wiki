AniBT is a public anime torrent tracker focused on anime and subtitle groups.

We want to give subtitle group members a platform for sharing their releases with the community, while helping users automatically find and download the content they want.

## Site Sync

Use these guides to configure AniBT sync settings for external sites. Each guide covers one site and its required Token, Cookie, or ID values.
