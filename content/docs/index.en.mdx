**AniBT** is a public BitTorrent tracker built for anime, with subtitle groups at the centre. We aim to give fansub teams a reliable place to publish, and to make it easy for users and tools to track new releases automatically.

This Wiki is split along two main use cases: **subtitle groups publishing releases** and **users / developers consuming the data**. Read whichever side fits you.

---
